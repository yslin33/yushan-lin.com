# Writing Image Attribution

## Images created by Yu-Shan Lin

- No attribution is needed.
- Do not add “by Yu-Shan Lin” or similar credits.
- The website assumes all original photographs, drawings, collages, and diagrams belong to the author unless otherwise specified.

## Images reproduced from external sources

- Keep the caption focused on the image itself.
- Add the attribution at the end of the same caption in italics.
- Do not include full citations in captions.
- Keep full bibliographic information in the References section only.

English format:

`Image title/date. (`*`Source listed in References.`*`)`

Chinese format:

`圖片標題。（`*`來源詳見參考文獻。`*`）`

This is the default convention for future Writing pages unless explicitly overridden.
